About

I am currently a research assistant at the Universidad de Santiago de Chile, working on the project Applicability of Shor’s Algorithm to Jacobians of Singular Curves directed by Nicolás Thériault

I was a PhD student at IMPA under the supervision of Eduardo Esteves .

Research Interests

My research interests lie in algebraic geometry. In particular, I am interested in applying techniques from quiver representations, tropical geometry, and (poly)matroids to the study of limit linear series. I am also interested in scheme-theoretic constructions arising in this context, including linked projective spaces and certain classes of Mustafin varieties.

I am also currently learning cryptography.
